GenAI Acknowledgement

How AI Was Used

Throughout my project development, I strategically utilized AI tools across several areas of my work. For the visual design components, I prompted AI for card display CSS on the style guide page, which I later repurposed for displaying colour cards as well. This approach allowed me to maintain consistent styling across similar display elements while saving development time. I also used AI very successfully for button CSS implementation, which produced clean, functional, and visually appealing styling that integrated well with my overall design language.

Form CSS development was another area where AI assistance proved valuable. The structured nature of forms aligned well with AI's ability to generate consistent styling rules. When encountering technical issues, such as bullet points not displaying correctly, I consulted AI which helped diagnose that the problem stemmed from previously implemented CSS rules‚Äîa troubleshooting approach that saved considerable debugging time.

For the more conceptual aspects of my project, I employed AI to help draft an ethical response. To ensure the AI had proper context, I uploaded my original analysis along with the feedback I had received and the marking criteria. This comprehensive input allowed the AI to generate relevant and contextually appropriate suggestions. Importantly, I thoroughly reviewed and modified the AI-generated content: updating it to be more appropriate for the assignment requirements, changing to first person perspective to maintain authentic voice, adjusting the CSS for better presentation, and analyzing all suggestions for relevance and accuracy.

How AI Supported Learning

Using AI as a tool enhanced my learning experience rather than replacing my academic effort. The process of formulating specific prompts for CSS development required me to clearly articulate my design requirements, which deepened my understanding of effective design principles and CSS implementation strategies. When creating card displays and button styles, I needed to evaluate the AI-generated code, understand its structure, and make informed decisions about its application in my project.

The experience of troubleshooting CSS issues with AI assistance was particularly educational. Rather than simply applying a fix, I gained insights into how CSS rules interact and potentially conflict with one another. This problem-solving process enhanced my technical knowledge and improved my ability to diagnose similar issues independently in the future.

Working with AI on the ethical response component of my project fostered deeper critical thinking. By providing my original analysis and received feedback to the AI, then carefully evaluating its suggestions, I engaged in a reflective process that strengthened my understanding of ethical considerations relevant to my field. The necessary modifications I made to the AI-generated content required thorough engagement with the material and reinforced my ability to articulate complex ethical concepts in my own authentic voice.

Critical Evaluation of AI's Effectiveness

Throughout my project work, I have definitely noticed that AI excels at simple, well-defined tasks but tends to underperform when asked to handle more complex, multi-faceted problems. This pattern was evident across different applications of AI in my project development process. For CSS implementations like card displays, buttons, and forms, the AI performed exceptionally well when provided with specific requirements, producing clean and functional code that required minimal adjustments.

The AI's performance in diagnosing CSS issues also demonstrated its strength in technical troubleshooting within defined parameters. When I encountered problems with bullet point display, the AI effectively identified the conflict with previously implemented CSS rules, providing a targeted solution to a specific problem.

When working on the ethical response component, which involved more nuanced understanding and contextual awareness, the AI's limitations became more apparent. However, it provided useful starting points based on my uploaded materials. This experience confirmed my understanding that AI tools are most effective when used for targeted applications rather than comprehensive solutions to complex problems.

Process and Documentation

Throughout my project development, I maintained detailed notes on my AI usage, documenting specific prompts, contexts, and outcomes. This methodical approach to documentation served multiple purposes. First, it allowed me to track patterns in AI effectiveness across different applications, informing my strategy for future AI utilization. Second, it provided the foundation for this comprehensive acknowledgement, ensuring transparency regarding which components benefited from AI assistance.

My documentation process included recording the specific aspects of CSS development where AI was employed, noting the contexts in which I sought AI troubleshooting assistance, and tracking the evolution of AI-generated content through my review and modification process. This thorough approach to documentation not only supports academic integrity but also reflects my commitment to understanding the appropriate role of AI tools in my work.

Ethical Considerations

Using AI in academic work raises important ethical considerations that I was mindful of throughout this project. Transparency is a key ethical principle I adhered to by maintaining comprehensive documentation of AI usage and providing this detailed acknowledgement. Following appropriate guidelines for GenAI usage, I have properly disclosed all instances where AI was employed in my project development.

I was particularly conscious of maintaining intellectual ownership of my work while leveraging AI capabilities. For technical components like CSS implementation, I ensured that all AI-generated code was reviewed, understood, and integrated thoughtfully rather than applied without critical evaluation. In the case of the ethical response, I was careful to thoroughly review and substantially modify AI-generated content to reflect my own understanding and perspective, maintaining the authenticity of my academic voice.

Throughout the entire process, I maintained a balance between leveraging AI capabilities and preserving my learning objectives. By using AI as a supporting tool rather than a replacement for critical thinking, I ensured that the final submission authentically represented my understanding, skills, and academic growth. This approach allowed me to benefit from AI as a resource while maintaining the integrity of my academic work and maximizing my learning opportunities.
