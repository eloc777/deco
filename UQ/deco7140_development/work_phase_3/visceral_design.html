Colour Scheme

My chosen colour scheme is displayed below. I focused on choosing warm colours that are culturally relevant so as to keep in line with the groups values of social cohesion and heritage. I also ensured the colour scheme provides sufficent contrast, keeping inline with WAG and the groups value of inclusivity.

--primary Red ochre (accent) #B04A02
--primary-bg Soft white (primary background) #F5F3ED
--secondary Soft orange (buttons/icons) #E07B39
--secondary-bg Warm sand (secondary background) #E3CBA6
--dark Charcoal black (headers) #22211F
--text Earth brown (body text) #7C4A02
--text-light Eucalyptus green (secondary text) #4C6A47
--accent-blue Deep blue (optional accent) #247BA0

Typography

I have chosen google's 'National Park' font. I have done so because it is simple, clear, and gives a natural feel to the website.
